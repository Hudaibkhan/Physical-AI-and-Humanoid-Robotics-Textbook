# Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics Textbook

## A Comprehensive Guide to Intelligent Robotics

This textbook provides a comprehensive guide to understanding and implementing physical AI and humanoid robotics. It covers a wide range of topics, from the foundational concepts of ROS 2 to advanced vision-language-action robotics.

Our goal is to equip readers with the knowledge and practical skills necessary to design, build, and control intelligent robotic systems. Through detailed explanations and practical examples, you will explore the cutting edge of AI and robotics.
